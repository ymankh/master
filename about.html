Our History

2018

A group of passionate teachers in Amman, Jordan, led by Yaman, Fatima, and Hassan, grew frustrated with the time wasted on repetitive administrative tasks in schools. They envisioned a solution to streamline these processes and improve education quality.

2020

They began developing TalimPro, a comprehensive school management system. With Yaman's coding skills, Fatima's design expertise, and Ali's administrative insights, they created a user-friendly platform that managed student information, tracked attendance, recorded grades, and facilitated parent-teacher communication.

2022

TalimPro was officially launched and quickly adopted by schools across Jordan and neighboring countries. The system saved educators significant time, allowing them to focus more on teaching.
